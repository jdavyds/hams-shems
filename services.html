GROWTH MARKETER AS-A-SERVICE

Every situation and person is different. Hams&Shem will pair your marketing lead with a Growth Marketer who has the experience that matches their marketing development needs.

Weekly activities for the growth-marketer-as-a-service

The above can range from 10 to 40 hours per week based on the size, maturity, complexity and growth stage of the company;

  • Hands-on, practical advice and assignments to get your Marketing going
  • Weekly meetings to discuss key initiatives, challenges, organizational issues, and communications opportunities
  • Ad-hoc access to your CMO Consultant as things pop up during the week
  • Continued challenge to build your Marketing function to drive growth.
  • Weekly Team meeting (including prep and follow)
  • Reporting to the CEO (1-1, based on Core KPIs)
  • Updating and acting on Marketing Dashboard and Performance
  • 1-1s with the team members (planning, coaching)
  • Working as part of the company executive team
