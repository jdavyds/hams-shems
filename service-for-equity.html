SERVICE FOR EQUITY PLANs

The Service Incubation model is an alternate funding model for startup that allows professionals to offer their services to startups in return for a minute equity (usually between 0.5% to 2%) in the startup.

For companies that want to grow across the various stages and cannot afford the cost of engaging the company, we evaluate the prospect and if we like the business, we will engage with a Service Incubation contract and usually will have one of our consultant seat in the company as a CMO-As-A-Service.

Service Incubation Equation;

Incubators invest usually ₦12m ($30k) for 10% equity which means that every 0.1% Share, will be an equivalent of ₦120m ($300) investment.

Hams&Shem service equity for investment formula:

For every 0.1% share = ₦120,000 ($300). So, 1.0% = ₦1.2m ($3,000)
